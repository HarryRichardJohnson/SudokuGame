Rules of playing Sudoku.
To win the game of Sudoku the GameBoard must meet all of the following requirements:
1)Each cell must contain a positive integer from 1 to 9 inclusive.
2)Each vertical row must contain all positive integers from 1 to 9 inclusive with no duplicate integers.
3)Each horizontal row must contain all positive integers from 1 to 9 inclusive with no duplicate integers.
4)Each 3 by 3 tile must contain all positive integers from 1 to 9 inclusive with no duplicate integers.
